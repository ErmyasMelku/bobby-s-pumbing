Eco-Friendly Tips

Save money & protect your pipes

Bobby T's cares about your home and the environment. Here's expert advice to keep your plumbing in top shape.

🚫 Skip the chemical drain cleaners

Those "drain cleaning" liquids are corrosive by design — they eat the clog, and then keep eating your pipes. If a drain doesn't clear right away, the chemical just sits in your pipe causing damage. Call Bobby instead for safe, proper drain clearing.

🥦 Use your garbage disposal wisely

Even with a garbage disposal, scrape large food scraps into compost or trash. Disposals weren't designed to handle big chunks all at once — over time, that builds up and causes blockages. Compost is better for your pipes and the planet.

🌊 Know your water service warranty

When we install water service lines from the meter to your house, we use products that come with manufacturer warranties — added peace of mind on top of our standard 1-year workmanship guarantee.

🔧 Don't ignore slow drains

A slow drain is a warning. Ignoring it means the blockage grows until you have a complete backup. Early intervention is always cheaper and less disruptive than an emergency fix — call us at the first sign of trouble.
